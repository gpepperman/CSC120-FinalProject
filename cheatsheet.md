
# SPOILER ALERT

## How to Play
- You arrive to Christmas with a list of "avoids" and a bag of gifts.
- You begin by sitting at one of the three tables.
- Each table has guests and objects to interact with.
- You can ask who each guest is to get information about other guests

## How to Lose
- If you insult a guest
- If you attack a guest
- If you say the wrong word to a guest
- If you throw an item at a guest

## How to Win
- You must give one guest their correct gift to win the game.
- You must be sitting at the same table as a guest to give them a gift.
- You must greet a guest before giving them a gift.

## Commands

### Table Commands
- "Sit {TABLE NAME}" lets you sit at a specific table
- "Unsit" lets you leave your current table
- "Who" lets you check who is at the table
- "What" lets you check what items are on the table

### Item Commands
- "Pick Up {item name}" lets you pick up a specific item on the table
    - also works with "Take {item name}" or "Grab {item name}"
- "Put Down {item name}" lets you put down a specific item on the table
- "Look At {item name}" lets you look at a specific item on the table
- "Eat {item name}" lets you eat a specific item on the table
- "Throw {item name} at {guest name}" lets you throw a specific item at a guest
- "Open {item name}" lets you open a specific item on the table

### Guest Commands
- "Greet {guest name}" lets you greet a specific guest at the table 
    - also works with "Hi {guest name}", "Hello {guest name}" or "Hug {guest name}"
- "Farewell {guest name}" lets you say farewell to a specific guest at the table
    - also works with "Goodbye {guest name}"
- "Attack {guest name}" lets you attack a specific guest at the table
    - also works with "Hit {guest name}", "Punch {guest name}", or "Slap {guest name}"
- "Insult {guest name}" lets you insult a specific guest at the table
- "Tell {guest name} {String}" lets you tell a specific guest at the table something
- "Who is {guest name}" tells you who a specific guest at the table is and gives you a hint about other guests's interests
